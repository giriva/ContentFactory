# ECONOMICS.md — The honest maths to £5k/month

## Monthly running cost at start
| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Claude API (2 pieces/day, Sonnet) | ~£3–6 |
| Everything else (GitHub Actions, Edge-TTS, Pexels, Canva free, WordPress free, Zapier free, YouTube/TikTok) | £0 |
| **Total** | **~£5/mo** |

## Why £5k needs more than ad revenue
YouTube RPM for most niches: roughly £1–8 per 1,000 views (finance/business niches sit at the high end). Ads alone at £4 RPM would need ~1.25M monthly views. Possible eventually — not the plan for month 3.

## The three-layer revenue stack (build in this order)
1. **Affiliate links** (from day 1): products/services relevant to the niche, disclosed. Realistic early: £1–10 per piece that gets traction.
2. **Ad revenue** (once eligible): YouTube Partner Program needs 1k subs + 4k watch-hours (or 10M Shorts views). Treat as a bonus layer.
3. **Own digital product** (Phase 2+): a £15–30 template/guide/toolkit solving the niche's #1 problem. This is where £5k usually actually comes from: 200 sales × £25 = £5,000. Sell via Gumroad/Ko-fi (free to list, % per sale) linked from every video and blog post.

## Milestone maths
| Milestone | Rough shape |
|---|---|
| £100/mo | A few affiliate conversions + early ads. Proves people act on your content. |
| £1k/mo | One winning format found. ~40 product sales or strong affiliate + ads mix. |
| £5k/mo | 150–250 product sales/mo driven by consistent content + email list. |

## Spending rules
- Upgrade a tool only when the free tier is the measured bottleneck.
- Any spend >£10/mo requires a written ROI case from the Master, approved by you.
