# NICHE_SELECTION.md — Pick once, commit for 90 days

A good faceless niche scores well on all four:
1. **Monetisable** — products/affiliates people actually buy (finance, software/AI tools, health & fitness, home/DIY, careers, travel hacks)
2. **Evergreen demand** — searched year-round, not one viral wave
3. **You can judge quality** — you'll be approving daily; pick something you can sanity-check
4. **Faceless-friendly** — works with stock footage + voiceover (skip niches needing a personal face/credentials)

## Strong candidates (UK-flavoured)
- Personal finance basics (high RPM, endless affiliates) — default in config
- AI tools & productivity (hot demand, software affiliates pay 20–40%)
- Home organisation / renter hacks
- Career & CV / interview skills

## Anti-picks
- Medical/mental-health advice (policy risk, needs credentials)
- Pure news/drama (no evergreen value, burnout)
- Anything you'd be bored approving in week 6

Change `orchestrator/config.yaml` → done. Then don't touch it for 90 days; the Analyst decides pivots with data.
